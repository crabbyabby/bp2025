About Us

Girls in CS is an after-school program aimed at providing a space for young girls to explore computer science. Over three weeks, students will learn basic HTML and CSS to explore website development and will have the skills to create their own personalized websites. Through this initiative, we aim to foster diversity in STEM by encouraging girls to develop technical skills and confidence in a supportive environment.


What you'll need:

— A positive attitude

— Willingness to learn

— Lots of energy and fun!!
