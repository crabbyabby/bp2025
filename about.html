More About Us


Girls in CS is an after-school program aimed at providing a space for young girls to explore computer science . Over the three weeks the girls will learn HTML and CSS and at the end they will create their own personalize websites. Through this initiative, we aim to foster diversity in STEM by encouraging girls to develop technical skills and confidence in a supportive environment.



All that you'll need is...
  • Chromebook for coding
  • Positive Attitude!
